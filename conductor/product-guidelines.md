# Product Guidelines

## Visual Identity
- **Colors**: Dark mode by default, high-contrast accents (Cyan, Yellow, White).
- **Typography**: Clean, tech-focused fonts (Inter, Roboto, or monospace for code-like elements).
- **Branding**: "Gillsystems" with the original 2008 logo.

## Tone and Voice
- Authoritative but human.
- "Commander" persona (Stephen Gill).
- No jargon, no corporate BS.
- Direct and honest.

## Components
- **Main Navigation**: Clean, accessible, sticky.
- **Connect Section**: Multiple ways to reach the Commander.
- **Footer**: Detailed links and community presence.
