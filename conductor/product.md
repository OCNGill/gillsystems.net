# Product Definition: Gillsystems.net

## Mission
GillSystems.net represents 30+ years of technology expertise. The mission is to provide technology solutions that serve humans, not vendors.

## Core Values
- Systems Should Serve Humans
- Zero Vendor Lock-in
- Maximum Empowerment
- Radical Transparency
- Build vs. Buy (Right Answer Discovery)
- Open Source
- LOCAL FIRST!

## Target Audience
- Individuals (Personal tech support)
- Small Businesses (Systemization)
- Enterprises (Strategic consulting)
